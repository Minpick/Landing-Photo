Stages

01

Pre-production

The video production process begins with pre-production, the key stage in creating a video. During this phase, the script is developed, actors or models are cast, locations are selected, and a storyboard for the upcoming shots is created

02

Production

Once the preparation is complete, the filming process begins, involving various specialists such as the director, actors, cinematographer, makeup artists, stylist, and others. The recorded footage is then handed over to the post-production team for further processing.

03

Post-production

At the final stage, the recorded footage undergoes meticulous processing by a team of specialists, including editor, colorist, motion designer, voice-over artist, and sound designer.
